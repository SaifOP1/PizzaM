Alfredo

The alphabetical first Pizza of them all with an Alpha status.

Items included in this Kit

  • Pizza Box contains the ready to use (not to eat now, its eatable after cooking) pizza dough (sealed and frozen) pre rolled on a disposable oven tray.
  • Instruction manual (the one you are reading now)
  • Labeled containers (Pizza of choice toppings)

General very extremely important instructions (optional) it is up to you to follow them really:

  • Turn up the oven temperature to the Max, 240 C and above for optimal results.
  • Let the dough rest for 30 minutes in room temperature before use and keep it sealed.
  • Wash your hands with soup or if you are like me just wear gloves and get right into it.

Ingredients check list: make sure that you have everything in front of you

  • White Sauce container
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese container
  • Sliced fresh mushrooms container
  • Grilled chicken cubes container
  • Parmesan cheese container
  • Almost forgot, sealed frozen pizza dough in (of course) container

Easy to follow steps:

  1. Open the dough seal, and be dramatic while doing so.
  2. Spread that creamy juicy white all over the dough, keep away from the edges because we don’t want any leakage, do we?
  3. Drop the fresh mushroom slices randomly on top of the sauce, make sure they look nice and neat.
  4. Do the same for the chicken cubes.
  5. Do the same for the Mozzarella cheese
  6. Do the same for the Parmesan cheese, but this time do it like chef Nasrat and look at your partner’s reaction.
  7. Now you can put the pizza tray in the oven like a boss and leave it there for 10 to 15 minutes, do not forget it in there because it might burn so don’t be lazy, go check on it from time to time.
  8. When you think its ready, take it out of the oven. Note: if its burning hot then its good, if its cold then you are an idiot, but it happens, trust me I know.
  9. Enjoy cutting your pizza now, eating is optional.
